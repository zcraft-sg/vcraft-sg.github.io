Cookies Policy

This policy explains how we use cookies and similar tools on vcraft.sg. We operate from Singapore and follow local guidance on privacy and data protection.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to remember your preferences and improve a website.

How We Use Cookies

We currently do not set any tracking or advertising cookies. We use only essential cookies that may be provided by our hosting platform to keep the site running safely.

Managing Cookies

You can clear or block cookies through your browser settings. Doing so may affect how some features work, but the site will still be usable.

Updates

If our use of cookies changes, we will update this page and note the new date.
